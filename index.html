Drag and drop routers to add them to the network.


Instead of drag and drop, you can also click and hold shift while continuously clicking to add many routers.


Pressing 1, 2 or 3 will respectively select CE, LER or LSR, this works with the holding of shift to insert routers.


CEs can only connect to LERs

LERs can only connect to CEs and LSRs

LSRs can only connect to LERs and other LSRs


Pressing Space will pause/resume the packet animations.


Pressing Tab will open the simulation panel.


Hold Shift, then click and drag between routers to create links


Hold alt, then click and drag between CE's to create destinations


Double click a router or link to configure it.


The clear button will delete the entire network.


The export button will download your network as a .json file


Packets colored red do not have a label, and packets colored blue have a label


If you are experiencing packets going offroad, please lower the max packets count.
